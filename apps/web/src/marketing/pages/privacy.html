How KemisDigital collects, uses, and protects information shared through kemisdigital.com and our forms. Last updated: July 2026.

Who we are

KemisDigital ("we", "us", "our") designs and builds software platforms for Bahamian businesses. This privacy policy explains how we handle information collected through kemisdigital.com, including contact and Get Paid Online application forms.

Information we collect

When you use our site or submit a form, we may collect:

  • Contact details — name, email address, phone number, company or business name, and your role.
  • Business context — website URL, payment processor details, project needs, and other information you share in free-text fields.
  • Technical information — source URL, browser user agent, and CAPTCHA verification tokens used to protect forms from abuse.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information (such as ID numbers or payment card details) through this site. Please do not include that type of information in free-text fields.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Review your enquiry or application and prepare a response.
  • Follow up by email or phone.
  • Plan and improve our services and internal processes.
  • Keep basic records for operational, security, and legal purposes.
  • Protect our forms from automated abuse and spam.

Where your data is stored

Form submissions may be stored securely in our database providers and emailed to our team inbox. Infrastructure, email, and security providers process data on our behalf and are bound by their own security and privacy commitments.

How long we keep information

We keep submission data for as long as it is useful for evaluating projects, maintaining client relationships, and meeting our legal or regulatory obligations. If you would like us to remove your information from our records, contact us at frontdesk@kemisdigital.com.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service providers who host our infrastructure, email, CAPTCHA, and analytics — strictly to provide their services to us.
  • Professional advisors or authorities if required to comply with law, enforce our agreements, or protect our rights.

Cookies and analytics

Our site may use basic analytics, logging, and security tools to understand how visitors use kemisdigital.com and to keep the platform secure. Cookies or similar technologies are used to support site functionality, performance, and form protection — not for third-party advertising.

Your choices

You can request that we:

  • Provide a copy of the information you have shared with us.
  • Correct information that is inaccurate or outdated.
  • Delete information we no longer need for the purposes described above.

Email frontdesk@kemisdigital.com and we will respond as soon as we reasonably can.
